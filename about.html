About the MIS & BA Club

The Management Information Systems and Business Analytics Club at the University of Minnesota Duluth is dedicated to connecting students with industry professionals, building technical skills, and fostering a community of future business leaders. Whether you are exploring majors or preparing for graduation, our club offers hands-on projects, networking events, and workshops designed to give you a competitive edge.
